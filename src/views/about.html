Welcome to YFN

We are a collective of young entrepeneurs and startup enthusiasts

We share a passion for "tackling things ourselves".

We don't rely on the world to improve, we are the change we have been waiting for.

FAQ - What do you want to now?

  • Do I have to have already founded a company to become a member?

    No, you don't have to. With us, it's less about the start-up behind it and much more about your personality. That's why we have everything in our network: from absolute newcomers who are just trying out their first business idea to founders who have already completed several rounds of financing. It is important to us that we can all learn from each other.

  • How can I become a member?

    It's very simple. You can simply register on this page and create an account. We'll take a look at your profile and decide whether you're a good fit for us. As soon as your account is confirmed, you can browse our network and register for events.

  • Do I have to have taken part in "StartUp Teens" or "Jugend gründet" to become a member?

    No. Although many of our members have taken part in various start-up competitions, this is not a must.

  • What does it cost to take part in YFN events?

    A ticket for the YFN Event #5 will cost 40 €. We do not earn anything from it; the entrance fee is only to cover part of the costs. Included in the 40 € are the entrance fee, all drinks, snacks and all food for the event weekend.

  • Does the Young Founders Network earn money?

    No. The Young Founders Network is explicitly non-profit. Our goal is to create a cool environment to learn new things, exchange ideas with like-minded people and meet new people.

  • Wie meldet man sich zum YFN Event an?

    Ungefähr 3 Wochen vor dem Event öffnen wir dann die Registrierung und du kannst dir auf der jeweiligen Event-Seite(YFN Event #5) ein Ticket kaufen. Wir bitten dich nur ein Ticket zu kaufen, wenn du auch wirklich kommen kannst, damit du niemanden anderen einen Platz wegnimmst.

  • Is the YFN linked to a political party?

    No. The Young Founders Network is independent of any party politics. We do represent various political demands, such as the desire for a simplified, less bureaucratic start-up process, but we are explicitly not a political organization.

  • Where can I find out when & where the next YFN event will take place?

    We organize an event approximately every 6 months. We announce new dates in good time in our Whatsapp group. Feel free to join if you want to stay up-to-date. Of course, you can also just check our website regularly.
